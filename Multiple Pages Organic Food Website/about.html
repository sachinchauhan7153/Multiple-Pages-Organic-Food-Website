About Us

About Us

In brief, we are your one-stop destination for all organic requirements. We home-deliver organic vegetables, organic fruits, organic rice, organic millets, organic wheat, organic pulses, organic spices, organic nuts, organic dried fruits, pure honey, organic sweets and many more...

Grow

We are a 30-acre farm that has not seen a drop of chemicals since 2017. We follow Natural Farming practices inspired by Padma Shri Subhash Palekar's Natural Farming Methodology. From Organic Ancient Basmati to Organic Sharbati Wheat, from Organic Ancient Red Jowar to Organic Bajri (Desi Bajra), from Organic Tomatoes to Organic Carrots, from Organic Sugarcane to Organic Turmeric, from Organic Papaya to Organic Banana to Organic Guava, we have experimented with everything in this journey approaching a decade.

For Our Customers

We want to provide quality, healthy and chemical-free food (including organic fruits, vegetables, rice, flours, cereals, millets, pulses, oils, ghee, sweeteners, spices, honey and processed foods). We strive to provide freshest produce and get fresh supplies from our farm thrice a week even as produce from various farmers comes to us on a daily frequency. We donâ€™t sell most veggies beyond two days of harvesting; leafy vegetables are never sold beyond this deadline. We have an absolute commitment to never use any chemical-based fertilizers or pesticides or insecticides or fungicides or herbicides at our farms..

For Our World

Our world is our families, our employees, our farmers and our laborers in particular and the populace in general. We have learnt gradually the climate change effects of wrong form of agriculture passed off as modern agriculture including modern fertilizers, irrigation techniques and seeds. We are, in a way, on a a mission to turn the clock back a little - heirloom seeds and native seeds wherever practical, zero chemical-based fertilizer, no pesticides / insecticides / fungicides / herbicides and more manual methods of harvesting instead of big machines. Monoculture, a significant cause of climate change, has to pave the way for trees on farms so that birds have a place to live, fruits have a permanent space with veggies and cereals and our farm labor eats more nutritious food.

We Deliver

When you look for chemical-free, adulteration-free, organic produce, we bring it to your home. Whether grown by us or sourced from organic farmers across India, we own up on authenticity and our own dedicated delivery team delivers our organic produce to you every day. For now, we serve Delhi, Gurugram, Noida and Faridabad with all our produce including fresh organic vegetables, organic fruits and other organic grocery. We also deliver non-perishable organic grocery across India.
